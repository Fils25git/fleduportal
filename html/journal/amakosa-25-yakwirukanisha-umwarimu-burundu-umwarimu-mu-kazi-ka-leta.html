Mu kazi k'uburezi, umwarimu agomba kwitwararika no gukurikiza amahame ngenderwaho y'umwuga. Hari amakosa atandukanye ashobora gukorwa n'umwarimu, amwe muri yo akaba atuma ahabwa ibihano byoroheje nko kwihanangirizwa cyangwa kugawa, mu gihe ayandi ashobora gutuma ahagarikwa by'agateganyo. Amakosa akomeye kurushaho ni yo ateganywa mu ngingo ya 67, aho umukozi ashobora guhita yirukanwa ku kazi nta gutegereza ibindi bihano.

Posted on March 12, 2025
Written by Fils Lambert IRATURINZE

Aya ni yo makosa 25 Ashobora Gutuma Umukozi Yirukanwa ku Kazi

  1. Gutakariza akazi nta mpamvu zumvikana cyangwa nta ruhushya nibura iminsi itanu y’akazi ikurikirana.
  2. Guhimba cyangwa gukwirakwiza amakuru agamije gutera ubwoba, gukangisha, kubiba inzangano, cyangwa gucamo ibice abakozi cyangwa abanyeshuri.
  3. Gukoresha uburyo butari ubwa kinyamwuga mu gutanga cyangwa guhindura amanota y’abanyeshuri.
  4. Kudakurikiza ibipimo ngenderwaho mu kwigisha no gutanga ibizamini.
  5. Kuba yarakatiwe burundu n’urukiko igihano cy’igifungo kingana cyangwa kirenze amezi atandatu.
  6. Gukoresha imvugo cyangwa ubundi buryo bushyigikira ingengabitekerezo ya jenoside cyangwa irindi vangura.
  7. Guhindura ibikubiye muri dosiye y’akazi cyangwa iy’undi mukozi binyuranyije n’amategeko.
  8. Gutanga ibyangombwa mpimbano kugira ngo ahabwe akazi.
  9. Gukoresha nabi umutungo w’ishuri no kuwunyereza.
  10. Gukoresha imbaraga cyangwa iterabwoba ku banyeshuri, ababyeyi, cyangwa abandi bakozi.
  11. Gufata ku ngufu cyangwa gukoresha imibonano mpuzabitsina ku gahato umunyeshuri, umukozi, cyangwa undi muntu.
  12. Gusambanya umwana cyangwa umunyeshuri.
  13. Gukoresha ibiyobyabwenge cyangwa kubikwirakwiza ku kazi.
  14. Gukubita umunyeshuri cyangwa undi mukozi ku buryo bubangamira ubuzima bwe cyangwa bumusigira ubumuga.
  15. Gukoresha ibikoresho by’ishuri ku nyungu ze bwite atabyemerewe.
  16. Kuba impamvu y’impanuka zikomeye kubera uburangare mu kazi.
  17. Kwangiza imitungo y’ishuri ku bushake.
  18. Gukoresha umwanya w’akazi mu bikorwa bitari ibya kinyamwuga.
  19. Kunanirwa gukurikirana imyitwarire y’abanyeshuri mu gihe cy’amasomo cyangwa ingendo.
  20. Kwanga gutangaza amakosa akomeye y’umukozi mugenzi we iyo ayamenye.
  21. Kudashyira mu bikorwa amabwiriza cyangwa imyanzuro y’inzego zishinzwe uburezi.
  22. Kuba umuyobozi w’ishuri ariko rikagaragara nk’iryanduye bikabije.
  23. Gutakaza cyangwa kwangiza ibikoresho by’akazi bifite agaciro ka 500,000 FRW cyangwa birenze.
  24. Gukoresha telefone cyangwa ibindi bikoresho by’ikoranabuhanga mu ishuri ku mpamvu zitari iz’akazi.
  25. Kunyereza amafaranga y’ishuri cyangwa andi yatanzwe ku banyeshuri.

Umwanzuro

Nk'uko bigaragara, hari amakosa menshi ashobora gutuma umukozi yirukanwa mu rwego rw'uburezi. Ni ingenzi ko abarimu n'abandi bakozi b'amashuri bubahiriza amahame ngenderwaho y'umwuga wabo kugira ngo birinde ibi bihano bikomeye. Uburere bwiza bw’abanyeshuri bushingiye ku mikorere myiza y’abarimu babo, bityo gukurikiza amahame y’umwuga ni ingenzi mu iterambere ry’uburezi.
