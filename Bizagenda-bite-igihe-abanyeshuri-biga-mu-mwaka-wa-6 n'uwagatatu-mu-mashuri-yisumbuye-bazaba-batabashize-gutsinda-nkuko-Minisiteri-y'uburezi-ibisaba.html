Bizagenda bite igihe amanota 50% Iteka rishya rya minisiteri y'uburezi riteganya kugira ngo umunyeshuri ave mu kiciro kimwe age mu kindi azaba atabonetse ku kigero gihagije ku banyeshuri bazakora Ibizamini bisoza amashuri abanza ndetse n'ikiciro cya mbere cy'ayisumbuye?

Posted on March 8, 2025
Written by Fils Lambert IRATURINZE

Hari mu kwezi kwa 11, ubwo minisiteri y'uburezi yahinduraga iteka rya minisitiri rigena imitsindire y'ibizamini bya leta. ugereranyije n'uko bimeze ubu, amabwiriza yari asanzweho niyo yari yoroshye kuko nibura umunyeshuri wabaga yagize amanota 20% muri buri somo,yabaga yemerewe gukomeza mu kiciro gikurikiyeho, ariko kuri ubu umunyeshuri asabwe nibura amanota 50% muri buri somo, cyangwa se aho atsikiye , akagira ahandi atsinda cyane kurenzaho.


Iri teka ryatangiye kubahirizwa mu mwaka w'amashuri wa 2023-2024 ku banyeshuri bari basoje amashuri yisumbuye ariko hagaragara gutsindwa cyane , aho abanyeshuri basaga 19,000 bose batsinzwe bikabasaba gusibira kugira ngo bazabone impamyabushobozi.nyuma y'amashuri y'isumbuye minisiteri y'uburezi yatangaje ko muri uyu mwaka w'amashuri wa 2024-2025, iri tegeko rizakurikizwa ku banyeshuri bazakora ikizamini gisoza Amashuri abanza ndetse n'ikiciro cya mbere cy'amashuri yisumbuye. bati "nta munyeshuri uzongera kujya yemererwa gukomeza mu kiciro gikurikiyeho, igihe azajya aba atagize nibura 50%" Minisitiri w'uburezi.


Ibyo byatumye abantu bibaza cyane niba abanyeshuri bose biga mu mashuri abanza n'ayisumbuye bazabasha kugira 50% nk'uko bisabwa kugira ngo bazabashe gukomeza . Ese Abanyeshuri nibura nka 60% batabashize gutsinda ngo nibura ngo bakomeze byagenda gute? Ese abatsinzwe bose bazasibira nk'uko minisiteri y'uburezi ibiteganya?


Bizagenda bite igihe bazaba batabashije gutsinda ari benshi?

Igihe abanyeshuri bazaba batabashize gutsinda ari benshi , uko byamera kose izi ngsruka zizabaho 1. Abanyeshuri bava mu ishuri baziyongera: Kubera ko abana bazaba batabonye amanota abemerera gukomeza abenshi uzajya usanga badashaka gusibira ahubwo barwana kudasubira ku ishuri kubera ko bazaba bumva batsinzwe.Ubusanzwe iyo abanyeshuri babasibije usanga bakunze kuva mu ishuri cyane, akenshi kubera bahita bumva ko badashoboye.
2.Ubucucike bukabije mu mashuri asoza ikiciro runaka:Ibi birumvikana mu gihe abana benshi bazaba batsinzwe kand itegeko rikaba ribadaba gusibira, abatazavamo bazajya gusibira mu myaka bigagamo, basangemo abandi bari bimutse bayijemo. ibi bizateza ubucucike bukabije muri iyo myaka.
Minisiteri y'uburezi igendeye ku buryo abanyeshuri batabashize gutsinda ku kigero gishimishije mu gihembwe cya mbere, yashizeho gahunda nzamurabushobozi ku banyeshuri biga mu mwaka wa nyuma w'ibyiciro byose.
Ese ubona hari icyo iyi gahunda izongera?
