Title: The Day the Pencil Ran Away!



One morning, Tom was ready to start his test. He opened his pencil case, but—Oh no! His pencil was gone!

“Where is my pencil?” Tom asked. He looked under his book, inside his bag, and even in his shoe! But it was nowhere to be found.

Suddenly, he heard a tiny voice whisper, “I’m tired of writing! I need a break!”

Tom jumped. Was his pencil talking?

The pencil rolled off the table and hid under the teacher’s desk.

Tom laughed. “Come back, Pencil! If you help me finish my test, I will give you a long rest!”

The pencil thought for a moment and then rolled back to Tom.

“Okay, but no chewing on me this time!” it said.

Tom smiled and started his test.
